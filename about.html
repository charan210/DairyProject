About Our Dairy

Freshness Delivered Daily, From Nizamabad to Your Home

Our Story

Nizamabad Dairy was started with a simple goal — to deliver the purest dairy products directly from farm to home. Our journey began in the lush lands of Telangana, where we partnered with local farmers to bring organic and unadulterated milk to every household. We believe in transparent practices, ethical farming, and healthy communities.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To ensure every customer receives 100% fresh, nutritious, and chemical-free dairy every day.

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted dairy brand that empowers farmers and promotes a healthier lifestyle.

Why Choose Us?

Farm-Fresh Daily

Milk delivered within hours of milking, with no additives or preservatives.

Happy Cows

Our cows are grass-fed, well-cared for, and raised in hygienic conditions.

Lab-Tested Quality

Every batch is tested for purity, safety, and nutritional value before delivery.
